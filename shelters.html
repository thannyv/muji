Shelters

Find a shelter near you in Berlin and start your adoption journey today! Our partners are super reliable and dedicated to giving animals the best care. With tons of heartwarming success stories, these shelters have helped countless pets find their forever homes. Join the fun and make a furry friend for life!

Berlin Paws Haven

Discover your new best friend at Berlin Paws Haven! We specialize in matching lovable cats and dogs with their perfect forever homes. With a warm and friendly atmosphere, our shelter is dedicated to giving every animal the love and care they deserve. Come by and see the magic of adoption in action!

Furry Friends Berlin

Welcome to Furry Friends Berlin, where every cat and dog is just waiting for a cuddle and a new home. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to ensure each pet is healthy, happy, and ready to join a loving family. Drop in and meet some of the sweetest pets in the city – your new furry friend might just be a visit away!

Berlin Tails & Whiskers

At Berlin Tails & Whiskers, we believe every pet deserves a happy ending. Our shelter is home to a wonderful mix of cats and dogs, all eager to find their forever families. With a focus on personalized adoption experiences, we help you find the perfect companion to add joy to your life. Visit us and see how we turn wagging tails and gentle purrs into lifelong friendships!
